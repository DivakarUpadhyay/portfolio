## Overview

The a16z crypto team compiled winter reading recommendations spanning fiction, memoirs, history, and technical works. The list includes contributions from team members across editorial, research, investing, and operations roles, plus selections from crypto founders attending their Founders Summit.

---

## Featured Recommendations

### Sonal Chokshi (Editorial)

| Book | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **The Loneliness of Sonia and Sunny** | A 650-page family drama by Kiran Desai, "rich with detail" and psychologically complex |
| **When the Going Was Good** | Graydon Carter's memoir exploring magazine publishing's golden age |
| **Empire of the Elite** | Michael Grynbaum's documentation of Condé Nast's cultural impact |
