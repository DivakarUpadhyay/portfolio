## Trend 3: Onchain Origination Over Tokenization

Rather than merely digitizing existing offchain assets, the focus shifts toward **originating debt instruments directly onchain**.

### Comparison

| Approach | Cost Reduction | Accessibility |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| Traditional Tokenization | 20-30% | Moderate |
| Onchain Origination | 50-70% | High |

### Challenges
- Standardization needs
- Compliance frameworks
- Legacy system integration

## Trend 4: Crypto-Native RWA Approaches

![RWA Tokenization](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1611974789855-9c2a0a7236a3?w=800&h=400&fit=crop)

### Perpetual Futures ("Perps")

Instead of straightforward asset digitization, perpetual futures offer:

1. **Deeper liquidity** than spot markets
2. **Built-in leverage** for sophisticated traders
3. **Better price discovery** mechanisms

### Best Candidates for Perpification

| Asset Class | Potential | Current Liquidity |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Emerging Market Equities | Very High | Low |
| Real Estate | High | Low |
| Commodities | Moderate | Moderate |
| Collectibles | Moderate | Very Low |
