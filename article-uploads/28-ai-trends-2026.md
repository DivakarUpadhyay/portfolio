## Trend 1: AI for Substantive Research Tasks

**By:** Scott Kominers (a16z crypto research, Harvard Business School)

### The Evolution

| Era | AI Capability |
|-----|---------------|

| 2022 | Conversation & coding |
| 2024 | Complex reasoning |
| 2026 | **Research execution** |

> "Models are now directly aiding discovery and solving advanced mathematics problems."

## Trend 2: Know Your Agent (KYA)

**By:** Sean Neville (Catena Labs co-founder, Circle architect)

### The Problem

> "Non-human identities now outnumber human employees **96-to-1**—yet these identities remain unbanked ghosts."

![AI Identity](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1531746790731-6c087fecd65a?w=800&h=400&fit=crop)

### The Bottleneck Shift

| Past | Present |
|------|---------|
| Intelligence limited | Intelligence abundant |
| Identity established | Identity missing |
| Humans primary actors | Agents primary actors |

### The Solution

Agents need cryptographically signed credentials linking them to:

| Component | Function |
|-----------|----------|
| **Principals** | Who owns/controls the agent |
| **Constraints** | What the agent can/cannot do |
| **Liability** | Who is responsible for actions |

### Agent Credit Scores

Similar to human credit systems, agents require:

- [x] Transaction history
- [x] Principal reputation
- [x] Constraint verification
- [x] Performance metrics
- [ ] Cross-platform portability (emerging)

## Trend 3: The Invisible Tax on the Open Web

**By:** Liz Harkavy (a16z crypto investment team)

### The Crisis

AI agents extract data from ad-supported sites while **bypassing revenue streams** funding content creation.

```
TRADITIONAL MODEL                  AI EXTRACTION MODEL
════════════════                   ══════════════════

User ──▶ Website ──▶ Ads          AI Agent ──▶ Website
    │                   │                         │
    └── Content ◀──────┘          Content ◀──────┘
                                          │
Revenue ✅                         Revenue ❌

════════════════════════════════════════════════════
```

### The Threat

This threatens the open web's economic foundation:
- Content creators lose revenue
- Quality journalism declines
- Original research undervalued
- Free information becomes unsustainable

### The Proposed Fix

| From | To |
|------|-----|
| Static licensing | Real-time, usage-based compensation |
| One-time payments | Blockchain-enabled nanopayments |
| Unknown attribution | Transparent attribution standards |
